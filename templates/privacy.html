Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last updated: December 15, 2024

Quick Overview

We collect minimal data necessary to provide our AI services, never sell your information, and use enterprise-grade security to protect your data. You have full control over your information and can request deletion at any time.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you use our services, we may collect:

  • Name and email address for account creation
  • Company information for business accounts
  • Communication preferences and settings
  • Payment information (processed securely by third parties)

Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information about your use of our services:

  • Device information and browser type
  • IP address and location data
  • Platform usage statistics and performance metrics
  • AI agent interaction patterns (anonymized)

AI Training Data

Conversations with AI agents may be used to improve our services, but are always anonymized and never linked to your personal identity.

2. How We Use Your Information

Service Provision

  • Deliver AI agent services and platform features
  • Personalize your experience and recommendations
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Provide customer support and technical assistance

Communication

  • Send important service updates and notifications
  • Deliver newsletters and educational content (with consent)
  • Respond to inquiries and support requests

Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance our platform
  • Develop new features and AI capabilities
  • Conduct research and development (anonymized data only)

3. Information Sharing

We Never Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Limited Sharing

We may share your information only in these specific circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who help us operate our platform (hosting, payment processing, analytics)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government regulation
  • Business Transfer: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • Safety: To protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or the public

4. Data Security

Encryption

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard protocols.

Secure Infrastructure

Enterprise-grade cloud infrastructure with regular security audits.

Access Control

Strict access controls and employee training on data protection.

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

1

Access Your Data

Request a copy of all personal information we have about you.

2

Correct Information

Update or correct any inaccurate personal information.

3

Delete Your Data

Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal requirements).

4

Control Communications

Opt out of marketing communications at any time.

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience:

Essential Cookies

Required for basic site functionality and security.

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how you use our platform (you can opt out).

Preference Cookies

Remember your settings and personalization choices.
