Most Common Questions

Quick answers to our most frequently asked compliance questions.

What personal data do you collect and why?

We collect only the personal data necessary to provide our AI services, including account information, usage analytics, and communication preferences. This data helps us personalize your experience, improve our services, and ensure platform security. We operate under strict data minimization principles and never collect more information than required.

How can I access or delete my personal data?

You have full control over your personal data. Access your account settings to view, download, or modify your information. To request data deletion, contact our privacy team at mail@intelcoreai.com. We'll process your request within 30 days and provide confirmation. Note that some data may be retained for legal compliance purposes.

Is my data shared with third parties?

We never sell your personal data. Limited sharing occurs only with trusted service providers who help us deliver our services (cloud hosting, analytics, customer support). All third parties are bound by strict data processing agreements and security requirements. We maintain a complete list of data processors available upon request.

What security measures protect my data?

We employ enterprise-grade security including AES-256 encryption, multi-factor authentication, regular security audits, and SOC 2 Type II compliance. Our infrastructure uses zero-trust architecture with continuous monitoring and automated threat detection. All staff undergo regular security training and background checks.

How do you handle data breaches?

We have a comprehensive incident response plan that includes immediate containment, forensic analysis, and stakeholder notification within 72 hours as required by GDPR. Our team conducts regular breach simulation exercises and maintains relationships with cybersecurity experts and law enforcement to ensure rapid, effective response.

Which regulations do you comply with?

We comply with GDPR (EU), CCPA (California), PIPEDA (Canada), LGPD (Brazil), and other applicable data protection laws. Our compliance program is regularly audited by independent third parties. We maintain certifications for SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, and are working toward additional industry-specific compliance standards.
